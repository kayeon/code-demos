CUTTLEFISHEZ:

Real O.G. Mutha######s of the Ocean

Cuttlefish can change their color, texture, and even shape at will. They can also squirt ink jets to deter predators. Male cuttlefish court their mates through architecture and/or advanced mimicry. They have huge brains, which allow them to spend their short lives, much shorter than those of comparably advanced vertebrates, contemplating their mortality, their spinelessness, and the absurd tragedy of existence.
